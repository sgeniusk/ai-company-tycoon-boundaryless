# Game Vision — AI Company Tycoon: Boundaryless

## Title

**AI Company Tycoon: Boundaryless**  
Korean: AI 컴퍼니: 경계 없는 회사

## Genre

- 2D Tycoon
- Management Simulation
- Incremental Strategy
- Event-driven Startup Sim

## Engine

Godot 4.x (GDScript)

## Target

- First playable: 10-minute MVP
- Expandable into: 30-45 minute Steam demo

---

## Core Fantasy

The player starts with a tiny AI startup and grows it into a boundaryless AI company.

This is **not** a generic office management game.  
This is a game about **reusable AI capabilities expanding across industries**.

### Key Feelings

- "This small feature can become many products."
- "Growth is exciting, but compute cost is scary."
- "I need trust if I want enterprise customers."
- "Automation is turning my company into a machine."
- "AI has no clean industry boundary."

### Core Mechanics

- One AI capability unlocks many products.
- One product can create users, data, hype, revenue, and risk.
- Growth creates compute pressure.
- Hype can grow faster than trust.
- Trust unlocks enterprise markets.
- Automation eventually makes the company compound on itself.
- The company starts as a tiny app maker and becomes a platform that crosses industries.

---

## Design Pillars

### Pillar 1: Boundaryless Expansion

AI capabilities unlock products across multiple domains.

| Capability | Domains |
|---|---|
| Language | Writing, Education, Customer Support, Enterprise Agents |
| Code | Developer Tools, Automation, Internal Productivity |
| Vision | Design Tools, Media, Enterprise Document Analysis |

### Pillar 2: Compounding Growth

Users, data, revenue, hype, and automation reinforce each other. But compounding creates new pressure.

### Pillar 3: Strategic Tension

The player must constantly choose between:
- Growth vs Trust
- Hype vs Safety
- Revenue vs Compute Cost
- Research vs Marketing
- Automation vs Control
- Short-term Cash vs Long-term Capability

### Pillar 4: Readable Numbers

Numbers must feel alive. Every resource change should be visible and understandable.

### Pillar 5: 10-Minute Fun

The MVP must show the core loop in 10 minutes: launch product, gain users, earn revenue, suffer compute pressure, upgrade capability, unlock new domain, face event, increase automation.

## Company Stages

1. Garage Prototype
2. Seed Startup
3. Viral App Company
4. Enterprise AI Vendor
5. AI Platform Giant
6. Boundaryless Intelligence Company

---

## Player Strategies

| Strategy | Focus | Risk |
|---|---|---|
| Hype Growth | Consumer products, users, hype | Compute cost, trust loss |
| Trust Enterprise | Safety, reliability, enterprise | Slow start, low hype |
| Automation Efficiency | Automation, optimization | Delayed market expansion |

---

## Success and Failure

**MVP Success (any one):**
- Users >= 100,000
- Cash >= 100,000
- Automation >= 60 with at least 3 active products

**Game Over:**
- Cash < -10,000 AND Trust < 10
