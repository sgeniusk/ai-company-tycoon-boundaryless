# Synthetic Playtest — Alpha v0.8.0 Competition And I18n

Date: 2026-05-15

## Persona Findings

### New Player

Sees that the world has other AI companies. The market share number is understandable, but the player may need a tooltip later explaining how it is calculated.

### Tycoon Fan

The competitor ranking provides a familiar management-sim scoreboard. Product-space claims make future product timing more interesting.

### Min-Max Player

Will look for a way to manipulate rival momentum. Current choices are light but enough for alpha.

### Casual Steam Player

Rival names make the world feel less empty. The menu is still dense, but the labels are readable.

### Harsh Steam Reviewer

The game now has a stronger AI-industry fantasy. They would still criticize the lack of animated payoff and deeper competitor counterplay.

### Accessibility Tester

Text is readable. The EN/KO toggle proves the i18n direction, but mixed-language UI should be cleaned up before public release.

## Recommended Next Steps

- Add tooltips for market share and rival momentum.
- Add explicit counterplay actions: partnership, acquisition, lawsuit, benchmarking sprint.
- Extract UI panels before adding real pixel-art assets.
- Expand locale coverage to resource, product, agent, and menu labels.

## Verdict

The competitor layer materially improves the fantasy. It is ready to support art direction and richer event design.
