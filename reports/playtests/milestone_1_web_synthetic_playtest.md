# Synthetic Playtest Report - Web Milestone 1

## Date

2026-05-14

## Build Context

- Milestone: Web Milestone 1 - Playable Dashboard Shell
- Benchmark: Game Dev Story iOS compact management loop
- Tested scope: first screen, product launch, next month, locked explanations, company stage, monthly report

## Executive Summary

The first screen is understandable and now works as a Korean playable shell. The strongest hook is the transformation of AI capabilities into products and company growth stages. The biggest current weakness is that product launch feels instant and functional rather than like a Kairosoft-style development/release moment with review, buzz, and sales feedback.

## Tester Findings

### 1. Male Web-Novel Strategist

- Works: "차고 프로토타입" to "시드 스타트업" gives a clear early progression beat.
- Weakness: The launch lacks a dramatic payoff line.
- Reference: Serialized growth fantasy needs a small cliffhanger after each month.
- Recommendation: Add monthly "업계 반응" snippets.

### 2. Female Web-Novel Strategist

- Works: Korean UI makes the premise easier to understand.
- Weakness: The company has no personality yet.
- Reference: Startup stories work when the team feels like a cast.
- Recommendation: Add founder/team flavor events later.

### 3. Male Literary Novelist Lens

- Works: Trust, hype, compute, and data imply a richer AI theme.
- Weakness: Ethical tension is not yet felt.
- Reference: The premise needs consequence, not only growth.
- Recommendation: First product should create both opportunity and risk.

### 4. Female Literary Novelist Lens

- Works: "신뢰" and "화제성" are promising social-pressure resources.
- Weakness: The game does not yet show who is affected by the AI products.
- Reference: Strong business fiction shows external stakeholders.
- Recommendation: Add customer/user reaction copy to monthly reports.

### 5. Male Commercial Comics Creator Lens

- Works: The board-like layout is readable.
- Weakness: Cards are visually calm; no punchy release panel yet.
- Reference: Game Dev Story makes small results feel like events.
- Recommendation: Add a compact release result box.

### 6. Female Commercial Comics Creator Lens

- Works: Korean resource names reduce cognitive friction.
- Weakness: The company stage panel needs stronger visual identity.
- Reference: A panel should tell the player's "current chapter" at a glance.
- Recommendation: Add small stage badges or office motifs.

### 7. Male Children's Author Lens

- Works: "다음 달" is clear as the first action.
- Weakness: There are many abstract AI resources.
- Reference: A child should be able to say "I made a product and people used it."
- Recommendation: Keep first 3 minutes focused on product, users, money.

### 8. Female Children's Author Lens

- Works: Locked reasons are helpful.
- Weakness: Some terms like 연산력 may need tooltip support later.
- Reference: Good onboarding explains hard words through use.
- Recommendation: Add resource hover/help after the core loop.

### 9. Tycoon Fan

- Works: Launch, month advance, and monthly report form a recognizable management loop.
- Weakness: No optimization choice yet.
- Reference: Game Dev Story becomes sticky when staff/product choices affect results.
- Recommendation: Milestone 2 needs product-specific trade-offs.

### 10. Min-Max Player

- Works: Starting launch has cost and compute pressure.
- Weakness: Not enough systems yet to judge exploitability.
- Reference: Dominant strategy testing begins once multiple products exist.
- Recommendation: Add at least 3 early product paths before balance claims.

### 11. Casual Steam Player

- Works: The first click is obvious and progress appears quickly.
- Weakness: The interface still feels more prototype than toy.
- Reference: Kairosoft games make menus feel playful.
- Recommendation: Add small celebratory feedback on launch.

### 12. Accessibility Tester

- Works: Labels are readable and Korean text is direct.
- Weakness: Dense resource strip could become cramped on mobile.
- Reference: Mobile-first management sims need short labels and stable tiles.
- Recommendation: Check mobile layout every milestone.

## Evaluation Lead Synthesis

Repeated positives:

- Korean-first UI improves clarity.
- Product launch and month advance are understandable.
- Company stage gives useful early progression.
- Locked reasons reduce confusion.

Repeated problems:

- Launch lacks emotional payoff.
- The screen is readable but not yet charming.
- Product outcome needs review/sales-style feedback.
- AI theme is present in resources but needs more eventful consequence.
