# Synthetic Playtest Report — Alpha v0.9.4 Opening Competitor Pacing

Date: 2026-05-15

## Scope

Synthetic review of the first-session feel after changing early rivals from immediate product-space claims to preparation warnings.

## Tester Findings

### New Player

- Improvement: the first few months feel less like falling behind immediately.
- Remaining P2: the game should explain whether a rival preparation can be countered.

### Tycoon Fan

- Improvement: the competitor clock is still present, but it now has a more satisfying warning rhythm.
- Remaining P2: competition needs more readable rival names in the timeline.

### Min-Max Player

- Improvement: month 4 becomes a clearer planning breakpoint.
- Remaining P2: deterministic preparation cadence may become easy to optimize.

### Casual Steam Player

- Improvement: fewer early negative signals before the first product payoff.
- Remaining P2: preparation text should later be paired with a small visual marker.

### Harsh Steam Reviewer

- Improvement: the game no longer looks like it is punishing the correct tutorial path with immediate rival claims.
- Remaining P2: the competition layer still needs a more dramatic "rival entered the market" moment.

### Accessibility Tester

- Improvement: `예고` is a clearer state label than only showing market-share movement.
- Remaining P3: color or icon affordances should distinguish prepare, claim, and pressure states later.

## Recommendation

Proceed to the next player-facing clarity pass. The strongest candidate is making the next growth fork after first release more explicit: productivity expansion, trust/safety enterprise path, or code/vision capability research.
