# Synthetic Playtest Report - Alpha v0.3.0

## Date

2026-05-15

## Executive Summary

The build now feels like an early management game rather than a static prototype. The office screen, product review badge, monthly event, and save/load buttons make the experience legible. The strongest improvement is the Game Dev Story-like release feedback. The biggest remaining gap is that products are launched instantly rather than developed over a short pre-release phase.

## 12-Person Panel Synthesis

Repeated positives:

- Korean UI is readable.
- Office/lab visual gives the project a game screen identity.
- Product review grade creates an immediate release payoff.
- Monthly event choices add strategic texture.
- Save/load makes the alpha feel more real.

Repeated problems:

- The office art is functional but not yet charming.
- Too many panels are open at once on smaller screens.
- Later event/upgrade data still contains English.
- Product strategy needs more differentiation.

## Evaluation Lead Recommendation

Continue toward a "10-minute alpha route" rather than adding more content. The next most valuable slice is a product-development/release cycle with a score reveal, because that best matches the Game Dev Story benchmark and will make the game feel less like clicking cards.
