# Balance Report — Alpha v0.4.0 Systems Prototype

Date: 2026-05-15

## Current Opening Loop

Baseline first path:

1. Hire 프롬프트 설계가.
2. Buy 프롬프트 노트.
3. Equip item.
4. Start AI 글쓰기 비서.
5. Advance 2 months to release.

## Observed Economy

- Starting cash: 10,000
- Early one-time spending: hire cost + item cost + product development cost
- Monthly spending rises because hiring also increases talent salary pressure
- Product revenue starts only after development completion
- Starter project completion is tuned to land before cash turns negative

## Risks

- P1: None.
- P2: Hiring and buying an item are both desirable, but the second purchase decision needs stronger payoff.
- P2: Compute is currently manageable, while cash is the dominant pressure.

## Recommended Next Tuning

- Consider a small "prototype grant" after first release.
- Tune second product costs around first product revenue.
- Add a recovery action, such as short-term consulting revenue or emergency seed funding.

## Verdict

The first loop is acceptable for pre-asset alpha. The next balance pass should focus on second-product pacing and recovery.
